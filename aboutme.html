Hey there, I'm Anh Quoc, your friendly neighborhood UX/UI Designer extraordinaire, on a mission to create digital products that even my mom can use without asking for tech support every five minutes.

After diving into this field for over a year, I've come to realize there's more to it than just making things pretty. We've got business goals, technical team wizardry, and deadlines dancing in the mix. The more I work, the more I feel the pressure of how much more I need to learn. It's like a never-ending knowledge buffet!

Currently, I'm flexing my design muscles at a HR tech company. Outside of design, you can find me doodling masterpieces, dissecting films like a true cinephile, and belting out tunes in my bathroom.

Oh, did I mention I live in HCMC with my parents and a gang of sassy cats? Life is an adventure, and now I find myself navigating it, moving pixels one by one.
